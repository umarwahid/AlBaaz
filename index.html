About The Company

Welcome To ALBAAZ GLOBAL FOODS®

ALBAAZ GLOBAL FOODS® is built for international food buyers who need dependable sourcing, clear product specifications and export-ready packing. We work across dehydrated vegetables, dehydrated fruits, spices and fresh fruits, supporting buyers with practical communication from inquiry to shipment.

Our growing export focus includes Saudi Arabia, UAE, USA, Togo, Brazil and Oman, with a target of serving 7+ exporting countries through consistent quality, careful handling and buyer-specific packaging. Whether you are an importer, wholesaler, food processor or retail brand, our aim is to become a reliable supply partner for quality ingredients and fresh produce.
